Thank you taking interest in our space!

Welcome to the Fox Lab Makerspace. We are a very community-oriented workshop that is interested in enabling and encouraging people to make whatever they want! When you come to the space, you can expect to find a collaborative, sharing, and generous community with a lot of creativity.

Becoming a member provides you with 24/7 access to our tools and workspace outside of public training, workshops, and classes.

Your membership dues directly fund our non-profit mission and allow us to do even bigger things for our community.

How Getting Starte ?

  1. Select you mebership plan.
  2. Contact and Submit your deatails to our management team
  3. wait for approval ( maximum 7 days )
  4. Show up and teach your class!

Membership Details

We allow 3rd parties to hold classes/workshops at the Fox Lab Makerspace but they must be sponsored by a member of the Fox Lab Makerspace ( if ther is no sponser for the event then need special permission from Board of Directors ).

There is no fee to host your event, just submit a request! We prefer that events be free and open to the public, but this is not required.
