Who We Are

Fox Lab Makerspace is a non-profit, shared community workshop and lab. We are an organized group of local engineers, makers, artists and thinkers who work together to provide tools and learning resources to the public.

What We Do

We use these resources to collaborate on individual and community projects in order to promote technology,art and science; while working and experimenting on innovative ideas to encourage learning within our community

Why We Do It

At Fox Lab Makerspace we believe that collaboration is a truly effective form of learning. To that end, our members and volunteers teach classes, hold unique educational events and collaborate on cool projects.

Why We Need You

Everyone has something they are good at, and teaching classes allows us to transfer that knowledge to others. If you have something you love, we would love to help you share it. and also Support our mission
